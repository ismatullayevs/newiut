Student Life

Get involved

A distinctive feature of WIUT is its incredible social life, where students actively participate in the widest range of cultural events, contests, attend creative and social clubs, and bring to life their own innovative social projects.

Realizing the undeniable influence of extracurricular activities on the development of soft skills, Social Engagement Section fully supports the students' initiatives.

KVN, Drama, Warm Heart, School of Volunteers, MUN clubs and societies make our students happy and us proud. Talent Show, Culture Fair, Fashion Show have become the landmark annual events that unite WIUT and the wider community.
